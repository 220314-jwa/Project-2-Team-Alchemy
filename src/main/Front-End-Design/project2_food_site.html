About

Welcome to Curious Appetite where you can get more information on the restaurants that you like. Have fun exploring our specially cultivated list of restaurant that we think anyone would like.


Each restaurant has a description, location, phone number, and a link to their menu so you can find it with ease. The restaurant will also have an up to five star raiting system.


Make sure to come back to this website to leave a review once you have tried out a restaurant of your choice. Reviews will automatically be anonymous unless you log in.


When you log in you will have the oportunity to save your favorites and leave a comment with your username.
